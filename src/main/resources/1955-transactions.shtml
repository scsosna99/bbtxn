    The Chicago Cubs traded a player to be named later to the Cleveland Indians for players to be named later and Sam Jones. The Baltimore Orioles sent Mike Blyzka (December 1, 1954), Darrell Johnson (December 1, 1954), Jim Fridley (December 1, 1954) and Dick Kryhoski (December 1, 1954) to the New York Yankees to complete the trade.
