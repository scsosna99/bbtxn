All Transactions

Transactions listed are from November 1 of previous year to April 1 of the following year.

  • November 12, 1923

    The Boston Braves traded Joe Oeschger and Billy Southworth to the New York Giants for Dave Bancroft, Bill Cunningham and Casey Stengel.

  • December, 1923

    The Philadelphia Athletics released Tillie Walker.

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Bert Griffith to the Washington Senators for Bonnie Hollingsworth.

    The Philadelphia Phillies selected Earl Hamilton off waivers from the Pittsburgh Pirates.

  • December 1, 1923

    The Pittsburgh Pirates purchased Buckshot May from Oklahoma City (Western).

    Oklahoma City (Western) traded Emil Yde to the Pittsburgh Pirates for a player to be named later. The Pittsburgh Pirates sent Ed Hock (June 20, 1924) to Oklahoma City (Western) to complete the trade.

  • December 10, 1923

    The Philadelphia Athletics purchased Max Bishop from Baltimore (International) for $20,000.

  • December 11, 1923

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Carl Mays from the New York Yankees.

    The Philadelphia Athletics traded Hank Hulvey, Harry O'Neill, Pinky Pittenger and $32,500 to Salt Lake City (PCL) for Paul Strand.

  • December 13, 1923

    The Boston Braves purchased John Kelleher from the Chicago Cubs.

    The Chicago Cubs traded Percy Jones and George Stueland to Seattle (PCL) for Sheriff Blake and Elmer Jacobs.

  • December 15, 1923

    The Boston Braves traded Hod Ford and Ray Powell to the Philadelphia Phillies for Cotton Tierney. Ray Powell refused to report to his new team on April 10, 1924. The Boston Braves sent Al Nixon (April 10, 1924) to the Philadelphia Phillies to complete the trade.

    Milwaukee (American Association) traded Al Simmons to the Philadelphia Athletics for Wid Matthews, Beauty McGowan, Heinie Scheer and $40,000.

  • December 26, 1923

    The Boston Braves signed Ike Kamp as an amateur free agent.

  • Before 1924 Season

    The Detroit Tigers signed Willie Ludolph as a free agent.

    The Philadelphia Athletics signed Elbert Andrews as an amateur free agent.

    The Detroit Tigers signed Charlie Gehringer as a free agent.

    Beaumont (Texas) purchased Dana Fillingim from the Boston Braves.

    The Philadelphia Phillies traded William Manning (minors) to Richmond (Virginia) for Bert Lewis.

    New Haven (Eastern) traded Stan Baumgartner to the Philadelphia Athletics for Frank Loftus and $4,000.

  • January 5, 1924

    Louisville (American Association) traded Wayland Dean to the New York Giants for $50000 and 3 players to be named.

  • January 7, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox traded George Burns, Chick Fewster and Roxy Walters to the Cleveland Indians for Dan Boone, Joe Connolly, Steve O'Neill and Bill Wambsganss.

    Louisville (American Association) traded Earle Combs to the New York Yankees for Elmer Smith and $50000 and a player to be named.

  • January 9, 1924

    The New York Yankees released Home Run Baker.

  • January 12, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox purchased Bobby Veach from the Detroit Tigers.

  • January 17, 1924

    The New York Giants traded Alex Gaston and plus option of Jack Scott to Toledo (American Association) for Joe Bradshaw.

  • January 31, 1924

    The New York Yankees traded Henry Lavallie (minors) and $5,000 to Omaha (Western) for Nick Cullop.

  • February 1, 1924

    Omaha (Western) traded By Speece to the Washington Senators for Jim O'Neill and cash and a player to be named.

  • March 1, 1924

    The St. Louis Browns signed Joe Evans as a free agent.

  • April, 1924

    Beaumont (Texas) traded Bill Black to the Chicago White Sox for Admiral Martin (minors), Lum Davenport, Red Ostergard and Joe Rabbitt.

  • April 14, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox traded Norm McMillan to the St. Louis Browns for Homer Ezzell.

  • April 16, 1924

    The Chicago White Sox purchased Ray French from the Brooklyn Robins.

  • April 21, 1924

    The Chicago White Sox released Amos Strunk.

  • April 25, 1924

    The Boston Braves released Bill Bagwell.

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Mike Gonzalez to the St. Louis Cardinals for Milt Stock.

  • April 28, 1924

    Minneapolis (American Association) traded Carl East to the Washington Senators for Showboat Fisher. Trade was voided and players returned on May 15, 1924.

  • April 30, 1924

    The Pittsburgh Pirates selected John Kelleher off waivers from the Boston Braves.

  • May, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Rube Yarrison to Portland (PCL) for Dick Cox.

  • May 2, 1924

    The Philadelphia Athletics signed Amos Strunk as a free agent.

  • May 5, 1924

    Kansas City (American Association) purchased John Kelleher from the Pittsburgh Pirates.

  • May 6, 1924

    The St. Louis Cardinals purchased Jimmy Cooney from Milwaukee (American Association).

  • May 16, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins purchased Tiny Osborne from the Chicago Cubs.

  • May 17, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Dinty Gearin from the New York Giants.

  • May 20, 1924

    The New York Yankees selected Lance Richbourg off waivers from the Washington Senators.

    The New York Yankees returned Lance Richbourg to the Washington Senators following previous waiver selection.

  • May 23, 1924

    The Washington Senators selected Curly Ogden off waivers from the Philadelphia Athletics.

  • May 25, 1924

    The Philadelphia Phillies purchased Ray Steineder from the Pittsburgh Pirates.

  • May 29, 1924

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Hughie Critz from Minneapolis (American Association).

    Los Angeles (PCL) purchased Walton Cruise from the Boston Braves.

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Chick Shorten from Minneapolis (American Association).

  • May 30, 1924

    The Cincinnati Reds traded George Harper to the Philadelphia Phillies for Curt Walker.

  • June 2, 1924

    Milwaukee (American Association) traded Wid Matthews to the Washington Senators for a player to be named later and cash. The Washington Senators sent Lance Richbourg (June 10, 1924) to Milwaukee (American Association) to complete the trade.

  • June 3, 1924

    Minneapolis (American Association) purchased Dinty Gearin from the Boston Braves.

  • June 4, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Gene Bailey and Binky Jones to Indianapolis (American Association) for Eddie Brown.

  • June 5, 1924

    The Pittsburgh Pirates selected Ed Hock off waivers from the Cincinnati Reds. (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • June 6, 1924

    The Philadelphia Phillies purchased Joe Schultz from the St. Louis Cardinals.

  • June 7, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Les Mann from the Cincinnati Reds.

  • June 8, 1924

    The Cleveland Indians purchased Frank Ellerbe from the St. Louis Browns. (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • June 10, 1924

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Cliff Lee from the Philadelphia Phillies.

  • June 11, 1924

    The Cleveland Indians released Guy Morton.

    St Paul (American Association) traded Chuck Dressen to the Cincinnati Reds for a player to be named later. The Cincinnati Reds sent Cliff Lee (July 22, 1924) to St Paul (American Association) to complete the trade.

  • June 12, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Lou North from the St. Louis Cardinals.

  • June 14, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Leo Dickerman to the St. Louis Cardinals for Bill Doak.

  • June 16, 1924

    The Cleveland Indians purchased Virgil Cheeves from Wichita Falls (Texas).

    The New York Yankees traded cash and 3 players to be named to St Paul (American Association) for Cliff Markle.

  • June 18, 1924

    The Boston Braves signed Frank Wilson as an amateur free agent.

  • June 22, 1924

    The New York Giants purchased Buddy Crump from Portsmouth (Virginia).

  • June 24, 1924

    The New York Yankees purchased Walter Beall from Rochester (International) for $30,000.

  • June 28, 1924

    The Philadelphia Athletics traded Rollie Naylor, Paul Strand and $10,000 to Toledo (American Association) for Bill Lamar.

    Memphis (Southern Association) traded Tommy Taylor to the Washington Senators for Doc Prothro.

  • July 1, 1924

    The Philadelphia Phillies selected Joe Oeschger off waivers from the New York Giants.

  • July 6, 1924

    The Chicago White Sox traded Doug McWeeny and Kettle Wirts to Minneapolis (American Association) for Johnny Grabowski and Leo Mangum.

  • July 7, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins traded Nelson Greene and $13,500 to Little Rock (Southern Association) for Jim Roberts.

  • July 12, 1924

    The New York Yankees purchased Al Mamaux from Reading (International).

  • July 14, 1924

    The Pittsburgh Pirates purchased Earl Smith from the Boston Braves.

  • July 15, 1924

    The Washington Senators purchased Ralph Miller from Reading (International). (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • July 18, 1924

    Kansas City (American Association) traded Pete Scott to the Washington Senators for a player to be named later, Chick Gagnon and Bert Griffith. The Washington Senators sent Pinky Hargrave (July 19, 1924) to Kansas City (American Association) to complete the trade. Pete Scott refused to report to his new team on July 26, 1924. cash and Pinky Hargrave returned to original team on July 26, 1924.

    The St. Louis Cardinals released Jeff Pfeffer.

    The Pittsburgh Pirates signed Jeff Pfeffer as a free agent.

  • July 22, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins purchased Bonnie Hollingsworth from New Orleans (Southern Association) for $15000 plus two players to be named.

    St Paul (American Association) purchased Cliff Markle from the New York Yankees.

  • July 26, 1924

    The New York Giants signed Leon Cadore as a free agent. (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • July 28, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Marty Shay from Worcester (Eastern).

  • July 30, 1924

    The Philadelphia Athletics purchased Jimmie Foxx from Easton (Eastern Shore) for $2,000.

  • August, 1924

    The New York Yankees purchased Hank Johnson from Bradenton (Florida State). (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

    The Philadelphia Athletics purchased Red Smith from Evansville (Three-I).

    The Cleveland Indians purchased Elmer Yoter from Saginaw (Michigan-Ontario). (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • August 1, 1924

    The Chicago Cubs signed Duke Brett as a free agent. (Date given is approximate. Exact date is uncertain.)

  • August 8, 1924

    Sacramento (PCL) traded Earl McNeely to the Washington Senators for a player to be named later and $50,000. The Washington Senators sent Wid Matthews (August 10, 1924) to Sacramento (PCL) to complete the trade.

  • August 9, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Herb Thomas from Daytona Beach (Florida State).

  • August 12, 1924

    The Brooklyn Robins purchased Tex Wilson from Des Moines (Western).

  • August 13, 1924

    The New York Yankees purchased Ben Paschal from Atlanta (Southern Association) for $20,000.

    Atlanta (Southern Association) traded Ray Francis to the New York Yankees for $5000 and 4 players to be named.

  • August 17, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox traded Oscar Fuhr to San Antonio (Texas) for Clarence Winters.

  • August 20, 1924

    The New York Giants released Eddie Ainsmith.

    The New York Giants released Leon Cadore.

  • August 27, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox signed Al Kellett as a free agent.

    The Boston Red Sox purchased Billy Rogell from Salina (Southwestern).

  • August 30, 1924

    The Boston Red Sox purchased Ted Wingfield from the Washington Senators.

  • September, 1924

    The Chicago White Sox purchased Spence Harris from Bay City (Michigan-Ontario).

  • September 2, 1924

    The Boston Braves purchased Joe Muich from Dover Beach (Eastern Shore).

    The Brooklyn Robins traded a player to be named later and $2000 plus a player to be named to Fort Worth (Texas) for Guy Cantrell. The Brooklyn Robins sent Billy Mullen (1925) to Fort Worth (Texas) to complete the trade.

  • September 3, 1924

    The St. Louis Cardinals purchased Tommy Thevenow from Syracuse (International).

  • September 4, 1924

    The St. Louis Browns signed Pat Burke as a free agent.

  • September 6, 1924

    The New York Giants purchased Grover Hartley from Columbus (American Association).

  • September 11, 1924

    The New York Giants purchased Frank Walker from Rocky Mount (Virginia).

  • September 12, 1924

    The Philadelphia Athletics purchased Tom Glass from Cambridge (Eastern Shore).

  • September 14, 1924

    Oklahoma City (Western) traded Joe Brown and Don Songer to the Pittsburgh Pirates for Buckshot May.

  • September 17, 1924

    The Chicago Cubs traded cash and 3 players to be named to Salt Lake City (PCL) for Pinky Pittenger.

  • September 29, 1924

    The Washington Senators purchased Wid Matthews from Sacramento (PCL) for $5,000.
