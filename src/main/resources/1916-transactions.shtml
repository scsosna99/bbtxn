All Transactions

Transactions listed are from November 1 of previous year to April 1 of the following year.

  • November 27, 1915

    Stan Coveleski sent from Portland (PCL) to the Cleveland Indians in an unknown transaction.

  • December, 1915

    The Cleveland Indians purchased Tom Daly from the Chicago White Sox.

    The Pittsburgh Pirates purchased Elmer Jacobs from the Philadelphia Phillies.

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Larry Kopf from the Philadelphia Athletics.

  • December 2, 1915

    The Philadelphia Athletics signed Whitey Witt as an amateur free agent.

  • December 16, 1915

    The Boston Red Sox purchased Sam Agnew from the St. Louis Browns.

    The New York Giants purchased Benny Kauff from the Brooklyn Tip-Tops for $35,000.

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Baldy Louden from the Buffalo Blues.

    The New York Giants purchased Bill McKechnie and Bill Rariden from the Newark Pepper.

    The Cincinnati Reds purchased Earl Moseley from the Newark Pepper for $5,000.

    The New York Giants purchased Edd Roush from the Newark Pepper for $7,500.
